<ul>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size:14px">Apple is <strong>transitioning to using their own Apple Silicon CPUs</strong>. This is a monumental shift for Apple. They’ve already demonstrated that these 64-bit ARM-based chips can easily match the speed of comparative Intel chips while drawing less power on other devices.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size:14px">The <strong>redesign</strong> is in line with the updates to with iOS and iPadOS.</span></li>
