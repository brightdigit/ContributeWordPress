<p>Good UI design creates a connection to your user gives them a pleasant, effortless experience using your app. This can mean the difference between an app that’s a positive investment for your business, drawing in and retaining customers, or a flop that you’ll write off when it becomes too much of a pain to maintain.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Avoiding this mistake is relatively simple: <strong>create time and space for both designer and developer to communicate directly in a way that works for them</strong>. It gives them room to develop a mutual understanding of both what the app can do and how that can be designed to create a great user experience.</p>
